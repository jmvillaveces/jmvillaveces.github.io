José María Villaveces

Developer and PhD Candidate

Profile

I am a computer scientist with a passion for developing open-source and reusable applications.

Currently, I am working as a PhD student in the computational biology group at the Max Planck Institute for Biochemistry (Munich, Germany). I am contributing to the field of life science and bioinformatics by developing software and tools for data management, integration and visualization of complex biological datasets.
